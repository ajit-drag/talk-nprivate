                                            Talk'n Private :
                                            ----------------

Talk'n Private is a great tool that can encrypt your plain text and and decrypt your encrypted text using the key. This tool uses the unique encrytion algorithm which is very much secure.

Instructions :
-------------- 
To encrypt your text follows these steps-

1. Open Talk'n Private in MS Window Enviorment.
2. Wait for Logo to dissappear.
3. Select "Encrypt Now".
4. Goto "Put the encryption key " and type your key to encrypt text.
5. Put the plain text in " Your text here " Column.
6. Press "Encrypt Now" to encrypt your text.
7. Press "Copy To Clipboard" to copy the encrypted text.

To decrypt your text follows these steps-

1. Open Talk'n Private in MS Window Enviorment.
2. Wait for Logo to dissappear.
3. Select "Decrypt Now".
4. Goto "Put the decryption key " and type your key to decrypt text.
5. Paste manually or Press "Paste From Clipboard" to paste the encrypted text.
6. Press "Decrypt Now" to decrypt your text.
